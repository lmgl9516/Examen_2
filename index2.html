Leonardo Gonzalez 고레오 高列奥。


Mexican athlete. Haidong Gumdo national team and current district selected archer

Leonardo Martín González López January 16, 1995 -


Haidong Gumdo

3rd Dan Black Belt in Haidong Gumdo , three times national champion and winner of the Gold Medal at the Haidong Gumdo World Championship 2012 in Daejeon South Korea and 4th place in the 2014 Mulimpia Pyongchang South Korea .
Practitioner for 8 years and teacher for 4 in Haidong Gumdo kham school graduating 5 Black Belts to its name, is part of the 1st generation of black belts from their school and the 8th nationwide and is the youngest member in the Mexican Association of Haidong Gumdo .






Taekwondo

2er Dan black belt in Taekwondo, medalists at the district level , participating in MDK five domestic cups and tournaments in the cities of Leon Guanajuato , Acapulco Guerrero and Morelos Cuernavaca .
Practitioner for 16 years and assistant professor at the school Haidong Gumdo kham for 4 years, part of the 3rd generation of black belts from their school and 205th at national level.

Archery

District team Archery member, seven times district champion team member and participant in the 2014 National Olympiad in Mexico City and the National Olympiad 2015 in the city of Monterrey Nuevo Leon .
Practitioner for three years at the school of Olympic velodrome , is part of the 1st team of the Federal District and the Venustiano Carranza town team.
